When It Makes Sense

Built for the moments that matter.

Fractional finance isn't a compromise — it's the right structure for specific, high-value situations.

"We're pre-Series A and need board-ready financials."

Investors expect clean, credible numbers. A fractional CFO prepares your financial model, cleans your books, and speaks the language your board needs to hear — without the cost of a full-time hire at this stage.

Startups & Early Stage

"We're mid-transaction and need senior financial oversight."

Acquisitions, mergers, and fundraising rounds need experienced financial leadership at the table. A fractional professional provides the expertise for the duration of the transaction — and no longer than needed.

Transactions & Fundraising

"We need a finance function built, not just managed."

Growing businesses often have accounting but not finance. A fractional Finance Director builds the infrastructure — reporting cadence, controls, team structure — then hands it over to your permanent hire when the time is right.

Engagement Models

Priced around your needs.

Fractional engagements are flexible by design. Here's how most are structured.

Model 01

Day Rate

Best for project-based work with a defined scope. You pay for the days worked — nothing more. Ideal for transactions, audits, or one-off financial builds.

Model 02

Monthly Retainer

A fixed number of days per month, agreed in advance. Provides consistency and continuity — the professional is embedded, available, and accountable on an ongoing basis.

Model 03

Project Scope

For defined deliverables — a financial model, a reporting framework, a fundraising pack. Priced by output rather than time. Clear scope, clear cost, clear result.

All engagements are quoted individually. Contact us for a tailored proposal.
